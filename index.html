Saints on the Lake

A Lakeside Parish Festival!

June 26-28, 2026

Join Us for a Weekend of Fun!

Welcome to the annual Saints on the Lake festival! Get ready for a fantastic weekend filled with live music, delicious food, exciting games, and wonderful community spirit. There's something for everyone, so bring your family and friends for an unforgettable summer celebration!

Daily Dinner Specials

Friday: Fish Dinner

Kick off the weekend with our famous and delicious fish dinner. A perfect start to the festival fun!

Saturday: Lasagna Dinner

Enjoy a hearty, homemade lasagna dinner that's sure to be a crowd-pleaser. Served with a smile!

Sunday: Mexican Lunch

Spice up your Sunday with a vibrant and flavorful Mexican lunch. A festive meal to close out the weekend!

Festival Attractions

Live Music All Weekend!

Get ready to dance! We'll have a fantastic lineup of local bands playing your favorite hits. From rock to country to oldies, the stage will be rocking day and night. Check back soon for the full band schedule!

Great Food & Cold Drinks

Beyond our daily dinner specials, you'll find classic festival fare to satisfy every craving. Enjoy burgers, hot dogs, sweet treats, and more. Quench your thirst at our beer and soda tent with a variety of cold beverages.

Fun for All Ages

Kids' Games: A dedicated area with fun and safe games for the little ones.
Bingo: Feeling lucky? Join us for exciting rounds of Bingo with great prizes!
Silent Auction: Bid on a wide array of amazing items and packages donated by our generous community.
