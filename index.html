Join Us for a Weekend of Fun!

Welcome to the first annual Saints on the Lake festival! Get ready for a fantastic weekend filled with live music, delicious food, exciting games, and wonderful community spirit. There's something for everyone, so bring your family and friends for an unforgettable summer celebration! Held at Racine Festival Site, South 5th Street, Racine!

Get ready to dance! We'll have a fantastic lineup of local bands playing your favorite hits. From rock to country to oldies, the stage will be rocking day and night. Check back soon for the full band schedule!

You'll find classic festival fare to satisfy every craving. Quench your thirst at our beer, soda and wine tent with a variety of cold beverages.
